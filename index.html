You Deserve a Customized Partnership
How We’ll Work Together
It’s not rocket science.

Introduction

We get to know one another! I ask questions about you. You ask questions about me. I tell you about LaSalle St. You tell me about your business: why you started it, why you’re passionate about it. All that good stuff.

Discovery

I begin to understand your pain points. I tell you how LaSalle St. can alleviate them. Most likely, we’ll identify a strong potential fit. If not, it will be obvious and I’ll point you in the right direction to help you get what you need. We’ll discuss some X’s and O’s: improving your operations, opening up your tech stack, your investment management preferences, how we’ll tackle specific compliance bottlenecks, economics and equity — we can go as deep as you want.

Meet the Team

You visit our home office in Elmhurst, Illinois. We lay the foundation for what will become a lifetime partnership by putting together a game plan to address the current issues you’re experiencing. By the time you leave — we have action steps that help us design a custom partnership, and you have confidence that LaSalle St. is the strong, collaborative support system your business needs.

Decision

We iron out the fine print. Figure out a way to get a deal done that works for both sides. If we’ve built our relationship the right way to this point, getting an agreement in place should come naturally. From there, we build a transition plan around your timeline and the real work begins.
